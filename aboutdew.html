This website is managed by Dr.Varodom Toochinda, often addressed by his nickname “Dew.” He received B.S. degree from King Mongkut Insititute of Technology, North Bangkok, Thailand, in 1987, M.S.E.E. degree from Kasetsart University, Thailand in 1994, and M.S.E.C.E. and Ph.D. degrees from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst, U.S.A., in 1998 and 2002, respectively. Dew now works as researcher/lecturer at the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Faculty of Engineering, Kasetsart University. His research interests include robotics, control systems design and implementation for industrial applications.
